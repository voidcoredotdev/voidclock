## Version history

### v1.0 — Pro
The original implementation.
Focused on long-running displays with a keep-awake function.
Built for tablets, kiosks, and wall-mounted screens.

### v2.0 — Classic
A simplified variant derived from Pro.
Removed kiosk-specific behavior to focus on a clean, general-purpose clock.
Introduced theming and basic configuration.

### v2.5 — Year
A focused experiment centered on year progression.
Introduced countdown-style time context (days, hours, minutes, seconds).
Designed as a single-purpose variant.

### v3.0 — Ultimate
Expanded time context with additional information.
Introduced year progress and city-based display elements.
Marked as experimental.

### v3.5 — UltimateProPlus
Refined Ultimate into a more structured, intentional variant.
Added weekday-based layouts and combined day and year progress indicators.
Clarified design decisions that previously appeared experimental.
